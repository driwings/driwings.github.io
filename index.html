FAQ - Frequently asked questions

How does it work? Contact us with your need or idea and we'll talk and develop it! We can iterate as much as necessary until you get the perfect design!

How much does it cost? It depends a lot on what you ask. Typically, expect it to cost something from US$ 30 for personal use, and US$ 90 for commercial use.

How is payment done? We typically use PayPal, but we can agree on other means. If you are in Brazil, it can be done via the banking system as well.
